Versatile Playmaking and IQ
  • While Curry is known for his shooting, his court vision and basketball IQ are elite. He is an excellent passer and playmaker, constantly moving without the ball, creating opportunities for teammates, and reading defenses at an advanced level.
  • He is also one of the best off-ball players in NBA history, constantly moving to create space, which adds another layer to his offensive dominance.
