Unmatched Impact on the Game
  • Curry has redefined the role of the point guard. Traditionally, point guards were known for their passing and playmaking. While Curry excels in these areas, his shooting ability makes him a dual threat. His deep shooting range forces defenders to guard him closely even when he's far from the basket, which opens up opportunities for his teammates.
  • His influence on the modern game is evident in the way NBA offenses have shifted toward more three-point shooting across the league.
